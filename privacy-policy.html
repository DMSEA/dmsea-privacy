Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how DMSEA Auto Post ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Facebook application.

2. Information We Collect

Our App accesses the following information through Facebook's Graph API:

  • Page Information: Page ID, page name, and page access tokens for Facebook Pages you manage.
  • Post Data: Information related to posts created through the App, including post IDs and URLs.

We do not collect personal user data, messages, friend lists, or any other private Facebook profile information.

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Publishing posts (text and images) to Facebook Pages you manage.
  • Verifying that posts were published successfully.

4. Data Storage

Page access tokens are stored locally on your own computer in a configuration file (config.json). No data is transmitted to or stored on any external servers other than Facebook's own servers through their official API.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any information with third parties. All interactions occur directly between your computer and Facebook's Graph API.

6. Data Retention

Access tokens and page information are retained only in your local configuration file. You may delete this data at any time by removing the configuration file.

7. Security

We recommend keeping your configuration file secure and not sharing your access tokens with unauthorized parties.

8. Your Rights

You may:

  • Revoke the App's access at any time through your Facebook Settings.
  • Delete your local configuration file to remove all stored tokens.
  • Request information about what data the App accesses.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
